What a light week actually looks like

You don't need to reorganize your life around this. Here's what "staying in practice" really looks like for a busy person.

The 20-minute week

Drop into one live practice class on your lunch break. Get your reps, feel the muscle working, get on with your day.

The big-meeting week

Something high-stakes is coming up. Book a one-to-one and rehearse the actual thing with a coach before you walk in.

The off week

Do nothing. Pick it back up next week. Nothing expires, nothing is wasted, and no one is keeping score.
